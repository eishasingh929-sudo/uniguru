# MASTERDB Status

## Sprint Goal
Move Balbharti MasterDB from a starter proof seed to a broadly expanded curriculum substrate supporting grades 1-10, Marathi and English medium, and multiple key subjects.

## Current Status
- Dataset expanded from 6 records to 2,560 synthetic Balbharti curriculum seed records.
- Coverage now includes grades 1-10, English Medium and Marathi Medium.
- Subjects covered: Mathematics, Science, EVS, History, Geography, Marathi, English, Civics.
- The ingestion manifest and proof artifacts are generated by `scripts/ingest_balbharti_masterdb.py`.
- Coverage validation reports 100.0% grade-subject-medium cell coverage across 160 expected cells.
- Integrity validation reports 2,560 valid rows, no duplicate record ids, and no grade/subject/medium chapter groups below the minimum chapter threshold.

## Known Limitations
- Curriculum content is synthetically expanded for sprint convergence and still requires verified real Balbharti textbook mapping.
- Provenance remains sample_seed; canonical authority is not yet granted.
